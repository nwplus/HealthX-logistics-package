Introduction

💊 The HealthX Logistics Package

About HealthX

Mission Statement : A chance for students interested in life sciences and technology to collaborate and tackle problems in the medicine and biology field.

The Package

This GitBook is a logistics package for HealthX. It is basically a step by step guide on how to plan and execute HealthX.

This GitBook is divided into 8 catagories of common hackathon planning items, and the planning process is summed up in the timeline. We reccomend first reading through the timeline to get a general sense of what items need to be completed and when. If needed, the items in the timeline will link to another page in the GitBook for further elaboration/explanation. Google Drive items should be accessible via the Google account here.

😊 Happy planning! - The nwPlus Team ✨
